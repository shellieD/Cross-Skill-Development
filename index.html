UI Cross Skilling Course

About

In an endeavour to help NatWest bank become a leading learning organization, Technology Academy is happy to announce the launch of the Cross-skilling on UI Development course. This course will encourage a poly-skilled workforce that's future-ready, and will mitigate the dearth of UI developers across the bank. This will be an MVP launched as Test and Learn.

The course will provide colleagues a comprehensive learning journey with self-paced sessions, instructor-led sessions with SMEs, hands-on exercises, and assessments as toll gates for moving on to the next level. It will cover core front end skills including HTML, CSS, Javascript and ReactJS.

Participantsâ€™ calendar will be blocked for one hour daily to provide a window for learning. The learning journey is comprehensive and is expected over 5-6 months including learning, sessions, assessments and project work.

To be eligible for this course, you must work at NatWest and be nominated by a team leader. Places limited to 20 participants. Please share nominations with David Rahman or Rashmi Manshani from Technology Academy.
